Grilled Mushroom Kebabs

Adapted from the book: Food for life by Anne Myers

Ingredients

  • onions
  • Browned diced meat(chicken or beef)
  • Button mushrooms
  • Red peppers
  • Kebab sticks
  • olive oil

Spicy Sauce Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 box of tomato and basil mix
  • Brown sugar
  • Paprika
  • Garlic spice
  • Spices for taste

To make the sauce

  1. Add all the ingredients to the pot and put on a low heat.
  2. Once mixed and it has started to thicken slightly. Put it aside to cool.
  3. Once cooled put it in the blender until smooth.

To make the kebabs

  1. Using a kebab stick.
  2. Place alternate ingredients, meat, onion, mushrooms, pepper and repeat.
  3. Once you have made all your kebabs, place in the oven until meat is cooked through.
  4. Serve with the yummy sauce and Enjoy!
