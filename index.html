Edible Mushrooms

White button mushrooms

When raw button mushrooms are a white or very light brown. When cooked button mushrooms turn a lovely golden brown. These mushrooms have a mild flavour. That becomes stronger when cooked. You can eat button mushrooms raw, fried with spices or added to a tasty mix with chicken.

Portobello Mushrooms

The portobello mushroom can have a large dark brown or tan cap, underneath the cap it is a dark brown colour. These mushrooms have a smoky, earthy flavour when fried or grilled. The scientific name for the portobello mushroom is agaricus bisporus.
